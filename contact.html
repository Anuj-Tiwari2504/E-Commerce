Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?

You can track your order by logging into your account and visiting the 'My Orders' section. You'll also receive SMS and email updates with tracking information.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 7-day return policy for most products. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Some categories may have different return policies.

How do I become a seller?

To become a seller on DesiMart, contact us at seller@desimart.com or use the contact form above. Our team will guide you through the registration process.

Do you deliver pan-India?

Yes, we deliver across India. Delivery charges and time may vary based on your location. Free delivery is available on orders above ₹499.
